# Travel and Expense Reimbursement Policy

## 1. Travel Approval

- Must be approved by reporting manager

---

## 2. Expense Categories

- Transportation (flights, cabs)
- Accommodation
- Meals

---

## 3. Reimbursement Process

- Submit receipts within 7 days of travel

---

## 4. Expense Limits

- Economy class travel is standard
- Exceptions require approval

---

## 5. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

- Personal expenses are not covered

---

## 6. Processing Time

- Reimbursements processed within 15 business days
