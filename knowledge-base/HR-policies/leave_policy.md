# Comprehensive Employee Leave Policy

## 1. Leave Types

### Casual Leave (CL)
- 12 days per year
- Intended for personal matters

### Sick Leave (SL)
- 8 days per year
- Medical proof required if leave exceeds 2 consecutive days

### Earned Leave (EL)
- 4 days per year
- Must be planned in advance and approved by manager

---

## 2. Carry Forward Rules

- Casual Leave: Not allowed
- Sick Leave: Not allowed
- Earned Leave: Can be carried forward up to 10 days

---

## 3. Leave Approval Process

- All leave requests must be submitted via the HR system
- Manager approval is mandatory before taking leave
- Exceptions allowed only in emergencies

---

## 4. Emergency Leave

- Employees must inform their manager within 24 hours

---

## 5. Leave Encashment

- Earned leave can be encashed:
  - At the end of the financial year
  - At the time of resignation

---

## 6. Public Holidays

- Employees are entitled to company-declared public holidays

---

## 7. Unauthorized Absence

- May result in salary deduction or disciplinary action
