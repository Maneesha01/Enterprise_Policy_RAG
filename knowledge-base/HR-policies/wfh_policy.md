# Work From Home and Remote Work Policy

## 1. Eligibility

- Up to 3 days per week work from home allowed
- Requires manager approval
- Fully remote roles must be approved by HR

---

## 2. Working Hours

- Mandatory availability during core hours:
  - 10 AM to 4 PM IST

---

## 3. Productivity Expectations

- Employees must:
  - Meet deadlines
  - Attend scheduled meetings

---

## 4. Equipment

- Company provides laptops and essential tools
- Employees are responsible for maintenance

---

## 5. Data Security

- Use VPN when accessing company systems
- Avoid unsecured public networks

---

## 6. Revocation

- Remote work privileges can be revoked due to poor performance
