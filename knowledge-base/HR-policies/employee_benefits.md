# Employee Benefits and Compensation Policy

## 1. Health Insurance

- Coverage for employees and dependents

---

## 2. Provident Fund (PF)

- Company contributes as per regulations

---

## 3. Gratuity

- Eligible after 5 years of continuous service

## 4. Performance Bonus

- Annual bonus based on:

## 5. Flexible Benefits

Employees can choose:
- Meal vouchers
- Internet reimbursement
- Wellness programs

---

## 6. Learning and Development

- Up to INR 50,000 per year reimbursement
- Covers:
  - Courses
  - Certifications
  - Workshops

---

## 7. Parental Leave

- Maternity Leave: 26 weeks
- Paternity Leave: 10 days
